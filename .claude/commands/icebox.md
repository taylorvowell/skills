Capture or manage future / nice-to-have feature ideas in the backlog at `docs/icebox/`. Alias of `/future`.

Invoke the `icebox` skill. `/icebox <idea>` captures (elaborating a brief idea into a structured user-story entry);
`/icebox list` shows the backlog; `/icebox develop <ICE-NNN>` starts building one; `/icebox done <ICE-NNN>` removes a
shipped item. If it's ambiguous whether you mean "build now" vs "file for later," the skill asks first.
